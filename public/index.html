      🧩 How to Play
      1
      Host or Join — One person taps Host a Game and gets a Room Code. Everyone else enters that code and taps Join Game.
      2
      Get Ready — In the Waiting Room, every player taps I'm Ready. Once all players are ready the host taps Start Game.
      3
      Solve the Rebus — A picture puzzle appears. Each image sounds like part of a word. Combine the sounds to figure out the hidden word or phrase!
      4
      Type & Guess — Type your answer in the box and tap Guess (or press Enter). The first player to get it right earns a point.
      5
      Use the Hint — Stuck? Tap 💡 Show Hint to reveal a clue for the current puzzle. Hints don't cost points — use them freely!
      6
      Final Scores — After all rounds, scores are revealed. The player with the most points wins! 🥇
      💡 Tip: Answers are not case-sensitive. "sunflower" and "SUNFLOWER" both count!
